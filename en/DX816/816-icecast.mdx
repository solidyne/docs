The **/AoIP** modules come from factory with **STL** firmware model, designed for **studio-transmitter link**.

The firmware can be updated to:

- Install a **new STL version**.
- Change from **STL** to **Upstreamer**, for direct Internet connection to streaming servers like **Shoutcast/Icecast**.
