## Using with mobile phone (Bluetooth)

To operate a phone on the console, it must be [paired with the Bluetooth](/es/DX816/816-telco) TELCO device.

### Quick reconnection

1. Ensure the **TELCO** fader is in **CUE** position (closed).
2. **Enable Bluetooth on the console:** hold down **BLUETOOTH** for approximately 5 seconds and release as soon as **CELL PHONE** lights up blue. The button remains flashing with slow intermittence.
3. Enable Bluetooth on the mobile phone â†’ the link is reestablished.
4. Incoming calls can be answered from the mobile phone or using **BLUETOOTH** on the console.

### Incoming call (mobile)

- Rings on the mobile phone and in **CUE** (TELCO closed). **CELL PHONE** flashes blue.
- Answer with a **short press on BLUETOOTH** (or from the mobile phone screen).
- **Preview conversation:** With fader in **CUE**, the call is heard through **CUE**.
- **Press TALK** to speak. The button is latched; press again when done speaking.
- **HOLD**: move the fader to HOLD zone, caller hears program, not on air.
- **ON AIR**: raise the fader to reach appropriate level.

<Info>
  In conferences between **mobile** and **landline/VoIP**, there may be **echo** in the remote journalist's return. It does NOT go on air.
</Info>

### End communication

- **Short press BLUETOOTH** on the console, or end from the phone.

### Resume on mobile

- On the mobile phone: exit **Headset/Bluetooth** mode by switching to **Speaker**.
- To return to console: switch back to **Headset** mode (varies by phone model).

### Making calls from mobile

1. On the mobile, disconnect Bluetooth to speak from the phone.
2. Establish the call.
3. To transfer to console, switch to **Bluetooth/Headset** mode.
4. With fader in **HOLD** position, mobile receives return from console but is not on air.
5. Raise fader to put on air.
6. To end: **short press BLUETOOTH** button or end from mobile.

<Note>
  You can also establish the call **without exiting** Bluetooth mode on mobile, with fader in **CUE** position and using **TALK** to speak from the console.
</Note>
