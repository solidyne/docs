## Using a Mobile Phone (Bluetooth)

To operate a phone through the console, the device must be [paired with the TELCO Bluetooth unit](/es/DX816/816-telco).

### Quick Reconnection

1. Ensure the **TELCO** fader is in **CUE** (closed).
2. **Enable Bluetooth on the console:** press and hold **BLUETOOTH for approximately 5 seconds** and release as soon as **CELL PHONE** lights up in blue. The button will continue to flash with a slow blink.
  <Warning>
  The Bluetooth module is powered by the **USB-1 or USB-2** channels.\
  If neither is connected to a PC, use an **external USB power source** (a standard charger) with the supplied cable.
</Warning>
3. Enable Bluetooth on the mobile phone → the connection will be restored.
4. Incoming calls can be answered from the mobile phone or with **BLUETOOTH** on the console.

### Incoming Call (Mobile)

- Rings on the mobile phone and in **CUE** (TELCO closed). **CELL PHONE** flashes blue.
- Answer with **short press on BLUETOOTH** (or from the mobile screen).
- **Monitor conversation:** With fader at **CUE**, the call is heard through **CUE**.
- **Press TALK** to speak. The button is latched - press again when finished talking.
- **HOLD**: Move the fader to the HOLD zone, caller hears program but is not on-air.
- **ON-AIR**: Raise the fader to reach appropriate level.

<Info>
  In conferences between **mobile** and **landline/VoIP**, there may be some **echo** in the remote journalist's return feed. This does **not** go on-air.
</Info>

### Terminate the connection

- **Brief BLUETOOTH tap** on the console, or disconnect from the phone.

### Resuming on Mobile

- On mobile: exit **Headset/Bluetooth** mode by switching to **Speaker**.
- To return to console: switch back to **Headset** mode (varies by phone model).

### Initiate Call from Mobile Phone

1. On the mobile device, disable Bluetooth to enable speaking directly from the phone.
2. Establish the call.
3. To transfer to the console, switch to **Bluetooth/Headset** mode.
4. With the fader in the **HOLD** position, the mobile device receives return audio from the console but does not go on-air.
5. Raise the fader to go on-air.
6. To end the call: **short press the BLUETOOTH button** or disconnect from the mobile device.

<Note>
  The call can also be established **without exiting** Bluetooth mode on the mobile device, with the fader in the **CUE** position and using **TALK** to communicate from the console.
</Note>
