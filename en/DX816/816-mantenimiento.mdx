To achieve optimal long-term performance from this console, follow the procedures outlined in this manual.

### Console Maintenance

- Keep the room clean and dust-free.
- Clean the front panel and cabinet with a slightly damp cloth or sponge.
- If dirt is stubborn, use mild detergent (for painted or wallpapered surfaces).

<Danger>
  Do not use alcohol, benzene, petroleum derivatives, or furniture cleaners.
</Danger>

<Warning>
  **Smoking is prohibited in the control room.**\
  Ash and dust are lethal to faders and buttons.\
  Food and drinks should also be restricted in the operation area.
</Warning>

The DX consoles use next-generation **conductive ceramic** faders:

- Lifespan \> **10 million operations** (~15 years of use) under normal operating conditions.
- No maintenance required.
- More information at [www.SolidynePro.com](http://www.SolidynePro.com).

---

### Preventive Maintenance

The DX816 and DX822 consoles use high-quality electronic components, eliminating the need for preventive maintenance routines.

- Handle the console with care.
- The rear connectors are not designed for daily connection/disconnection.
- Avoid connecting headphones directly to the console.

<Tip>
  If headphones are frequently changed, install an **external connection panel** to prevent connector wear.
</Tip>

---

### Replacement Parts

- All channel faders include connectors.
- Main faders are easy to replace:
  - Secured to the chassis with two screws.
  - Polarized connector for quick connection.

Once removed, any module can be repaired as all components are identified in the **Service Manual**.

<Info>
  We recommend purchasing the **complete module** and replacing it, given the low cost compared to repair time.
</Info>
