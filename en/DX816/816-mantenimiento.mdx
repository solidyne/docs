To obtain optimal results from this console for a long time, it is recommended to follow the procedures indicated in this manual.

---

### Console care

- Keep the room clean and dust-free.
- Clean front panel and cabinet with slightly damp cloth or sponge.
- If dirt is difficult to remove, use mild detergent (suitable for painted walls or wallpaper).

<Danger>
  Do not use alcohol, benzine, petroleum derivatives or furniture cleaners.
</Danger>

<Warning>
  **Smoking is prohibited in the control room.**\
  Ashes and dust are lethal for faders and buttons.\
  Drinks and food should also be restricted in the operation area.
</Warning>

DX consoles use new generation **conductive ceramic** faders:

- Service life \> **10 million operations** (~15 years of use) under normal conditions.
- Maintenance-free.
- More information at [www.SolidynePro.com](http://www.SolidynePro.com).

---

### Preventive maintenance

DX816 and DX822 consoles use high-quality electronic components, eliminating preventive maintenance routines.

- Handle the console with care.
- Rear connectors are not designed for daily connection/disconnection.
- Avoid connecting headphones directly to the console.

<Tip>
  If headphones are frequently changed, install an **external connection panel** to prevent connector wear.
</Tip>

---

### Spare parts

- All channel faders include connectors.
- Main faders are easy to replace:
  - Attached to chassis with two screws.
  - Polarized connector for quick connection.

Once removed, any module can be repaired as all components are identified in the **Service Manual**.

<Info>
  It is recommended to purchase the **complete module** and replace it, given the low cost compared to repair time.
</Info>
