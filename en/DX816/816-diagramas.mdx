## Power Supply

The DX816/822 operates with external power. It includes a **switching power supply 90-240 V** that works with any electrical network, even in areas with significant voltage variations. It delivers **regulated 28 VDC**.

The rear panel houses the **DC IN** power input with a **2.1 mm** barrel connector, positive on the center pin.

<Warning>
  It is recommended to always use the **original power supply provided by Solidyne**.
</Warning>

<Info>
  The Tally Light barrel connector provides voltage for the "On-Air" light. This connector uses a 2.5 mm pin to prevent accidental connection of the power supply.
</Info>

## Grounding

Grounding is accomplished via a **bronze terminal** that ensures chassis grounding. Use **2 mm gauge** cable connected to a **buried copper grounding rod**.

## About RJ45 Connectors

With the advent of **Audio over IP (AoIP)**, various manufacturers have adopted the use of **RJ45 connectors** and shielded multipair cable to replace traditional audio connectors.

- A single RJ45 connector contains **two balanced lines**, reducing the size of the rear panel and the number of connectors.
- The use of **structured cable** simplifies installation between distant equipment anywhere in the world, thanks to the wide availability of components and assembly tools used in data networks.
- Soldering is eliminated, simplifying and speeding up wiring.

At the end of the multipair cable, the connection to audio devices (microphones, speakers, interfaces) is still made with **standard audio connectors**.\
For this purpose, RJ45 runs terminate in balanced connectors suitable for the target equipment.

The balanced inputs of the **Solidyne DX816/822** console use **RJ45 connectors** and are wired with **shielded CAT-5 (STP)** multipair cable.

The **Solidyne MNGX816** connection kit includes all necessary cables for balanced inputs and outputs with RJ45, ensuring reliability and preventing connection errors.

<Note>
  Solidyne's RJ45 audio connections are **compatible with those provided by StudioHub** ([www.studiohub.com](http://www.studiohub.com)).
</Note>
