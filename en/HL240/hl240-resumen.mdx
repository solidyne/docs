## HL240-VQ Key Features

### Connectivity

- **POTS telephone lines**: 2 analog hybrids with adjustable rejection.
- **Bluetooth 4.0**: two independent channels with HSP / HFP / A2DP profiles.
- **VoIP compatibility**: through ATA adapters or Bluetooth-enabled VoIP phones.
- **Solidyne console interface**: direct hybrid send/return connection (2300, 2600, DX816/822, D612 series).

### Inputs and Outputs

- **AUDIO IN (Send to Lines)**: unbalanced, for mix-minus feed from console.
- **AUDIO OUT (Mix)**: balanced XLR output, delivers total line mix.
- **PH-1 and BLT-1 direct outputs**: unbalanced, pre-fader, fixed level.
- **Headphone output**: direct monitoring of main mix.

### Audio Processing

- **VQR (Voice Quality Restoration)**: bass reconstruction down to 50 Hz and treble up to 12 kHz.
- **NOISE Control**: noise gate to reduce background during conversation pauses.

### Operation

- **Illuminated buttons**: line status and ring cadence.
- **Built-in Talkback**: enables private conversation with caller.
- **Priority system**: 10 dB attenuation of line audio when local voice is detected.
- **Conference**: internal mixing of up to 4 lines (2 POTS + 2 Bluetooth).

### Performance

- **Frequency response**: 50 Hz – 12 kHz with VQR // 300 Hz – 3.4 kHz without VQR.
- **Signal-to-noise ratio**: 70 dBA with VQR.
- **THD**: \< 0.2%.
- **Hybrid rejection**: \> 40 dB (measured with 2 km artificial line).

### Construction and Power

- **19" rack format**: 1U height. Includes rubber feet for desktop use.
- **Internal regulated power supply**: 110/220 V switchable, 20 W consumption.
- **Surge protection**: metal oxide varistors withstanding up to 2,000 V.
