<Accordion title="CODEC – LINK">
  - **SIP:** Opus 48 kHz · Speex 32k / 16k / 8k · G729 · G722
  - **Icecast/Shoutcast Streaming:** MP3 · Opus 64–320 kbps
  - **RTP Streaming:** PCM 32–44.1–48 kHz · MP3 64–320 kbps · Opus 64–320 kbps
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connectivity">
  - Bluetooth 4.0
  - Gigabit Ethernet RJ45
  - Wi-Fi 2.4G / 5.8G (USB antenna included)
  - External 4G USB modem (not included)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Inputs">
  - 4 × Dynamic MIC (XLR)
    - Fader #3 controls MIC3 \+ MIC4
    - Level: –70 to –30 dBu
  - 1 × USB audio from PC (Fader #4)
    - Without USB, Fader #4 switches to analog stereo line
  - 1 × Stereo Line (Telephone/Line or Fader #4)
    - Level –10 / \+20 dBu
  - 1 × POTS telephone line (RJ11)
    - VoIP through external FXS adapter (not included)
  - 1 × Smartphone via Bluetooth 4.0 (50 Hz – 8 kHz voice)
  - Stereo PGM insert for external processor (\+4 dBu)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Outputs">
  - Stereo Program Out: Analog, unbalanced \+4 dBu
    - Max. \+14 dBu · Z \>5 kΩ
  - USB Stereo Program to PC
  - MIC Mixer Out for PC recording (–10 dBu / 10kΩ)
  - 4 × headphone outputs (16–600 Ω)
    - Level \+5 dBu / 50 Ω
  - Monitor speakers (replaces Headphone #1 output when on-air light connected)
  - 12 V / 120 mA output for on-air light (Tally light)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Recording">
  - Internal stereo MP3 recording
  - Over 100 hours storage (@192 kbps)
  - Resolution 16 bits / 48 kHz
  - Configurable bitrate: 64 to 320 kbps
  - Nominal level –10 dBFS · Dynamic range 80 dB
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Audio Performance">
  - **RTP PCM 48 kHz:**
    - Response: 20–18,000 Hz ±1 dB
    - Noise / Dynamic range \>85 dBA
    - Distortion less than 0.01% THD
  - **MIC to PGM Out:**
    - 20–15,000 Hz ±1 dB (with anti-pop –3 dB @30 Hz)
    - 20–20,000 Hz ±2 dB
  - **USB to PGM Out:**
    - 20–20,000 Hz ±0.5 dB
  - **Noise:**
    - MIC EIN –120 dBA
    - Line in S/N = 85 dBA
  - **MIC Compressor:**
    - Threshold 0 VU
    - Compression up to 20 dB
    - Attack 5 ms · Release 200 ms
  - **Distortion:**
    - USB In → USB PGM Out less than 0.02% THD @1 kHz
    - MIC –50 dBu → PGM Out less than 0.1% THD
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Phone Hybrid">
  - Response: 300–3400 Hz ±1 dB
  - Rejection: \>40 dB @1 kHz (rear NULL adjustment)
  - S/N ratio \>60 dBA
  - Built-in DTMF dialer
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Intercom">
  - Internal talkback microphone
  - Private communication with studio, headphones, telephone line and mobile
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Power Supply">
  - External switching power supply 90–240 V AC 50/60 Hz → 12 V / 1.25 A
  - Internal Li-Ion battery 2×3.7 V / 2500 mAh (with internal charger)
  - Approx. 4 h autonomy (Wi-Fi on, Bluetooth off)
  - Compatible with external Power Bank 12 V DC / 1000 mAh (≈15 h operation)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Insert External Devices">
  - Stereo PGM insert for external processor
  - Switched TRS 1/4" "Stream In" connector
  - Replaces internal PGM signal in streaming and USB
  - Level \+4 dBu
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Dimensions and Weight">
  - Width: 260 mm
  - Depth: 284 mm
  - Front height: 30 mm
  - Rear height: 95 mm
  - Weight: 2.4 kg
</Accordion>
