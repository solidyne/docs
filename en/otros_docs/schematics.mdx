In this section, you can request the **Service Manual**, which includes:

- Circuit diagrams
- Component layout
- Technical information for diagnosis and repair

Since some equipment has different hardware versions, it is necessary to correctly identify the unit before sending the corresponding documentation.

## Procedure for requesting technical documentation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send the information via email">
    Send an email to `info@solidyne.ar` attaching:

    - A photograph of the equipment
    - Equipment model
    - Serial number
    - Name of the station or company
    - Address and country
    - Clear description of the detected issue

    The more precise the technical description, the more efficient the assistance process will be.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receipt of the manual">
    Within **2 business days** after receiving the complete information, the **Service Manual in PDF format** corresponding to your equipment will be sent via email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Confidentiality of information

<Note>
  Receiving the Service Manual implies acceptance of maintaining the **confidentiality of the technical information provided**.

  The documentation should be shared exclusively with the technician responsible for repairing the equipment and must not be publicly distributed.
</Note>
