In this section you can request the **Service Manual**, which includes:

- Circuit diagrams
- Component layout
- Technical information for diagnosis and repair

Since some equipment has different hardware versions, it is necessary to correctly identify the unit before sending the corresponding documentation.

## Procedure for requesting technical documentation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send information by email">
  Send an email to `info@solidyne.ar` attaching:

  - Equipment photograph
  - Equipment model
  - Serial number
  - Station or company name
  - Address and country
  - Clear description of the detected problem

  The more precise the technical description, the more efficient the assistance process will be.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manual reception">
  Within **2 business days** after receiving the complete information, the **Service Manual in PDF format** corresponding to your equipment will be sent by email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Information confidentiality
<Note>
Receipt of the Service Manual implies acceptance to maintain **confidentiality of the supplied technical information**.

The documentation must be shared exclusively with the technician responsible for repairing the equipment and must not be distributed publicly.
</Note>
