**Audio Processing**

- Five independent bands of compression and limiting.
- Wideband AGC (WB AGC) and Multiband AGC (MB AGC).
- Intelligent clipper with loudness control.
- Configurable presets and user fine adjustments.

**Integrated Encoding**

- Built-in MPX stereo generator.
- Integrated RDS encoder.

**Inputs / Outputs**

- 2 balanced analog inputs (XLR-3).
- 1 AES/EBU digital input.
- 2 balanced analog program outputs.
- 1 AES/EBU digital output.
- 2 MPX stereo outputs (BNC).
- **542/AoIP** option for **streaming** input.

**FM MAP: Tuner and Measurements**

- Internal FM tuner for on-air monitoring.
- Real-time measurement of deviation, modulation, and signal levels.
- Correction algorithm for stereo separation optimization based on transmission chain.
- Spectrum scanning for monitoring other stations.

**Connectivity and Control**

- Ethernet port for IP remote control.
- Real-time monitoring of levels and status.
- Firmware updates without interrupting transmission.

**Standards and Compatibility**

- Complies with international FM modulation standards.
- ITU r1770 / EBU R128 loudness indicators
- ITU BS412 loudness control
- RDS 2.0 and extensions

**Operational Security**

- Automatic input switching in case of studio link failure.
- Remote support and adjustment service.
- Configurable alarms for out-of-standard deviations.
- 24/7 continuous operation without moving parts cooling.
