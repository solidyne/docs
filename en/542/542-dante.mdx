## Dante/AES67 Module

The DANTE/AES67 expansion provides 2x2 AoIP connectivity.\
DANTE connectivity is used in configurations where the processor is located at the radio station studios.

- Connects to the Dante network switch via RJ45 Ethernet AoIP.
- Automatically recognized by Dante Controller (Audinate®).

  <img
    src="/images/542-dante.jpg"
    alt="542 Dante Jp"
    title="542 Dante Jp"
    style={{ width:"51%" }}
  />
- The stereo output is configured from **AUDIO OUTPUTS → Ethernet AoIP**.
  - The source can be either the processed signal or the FM receiver.

    <img
      src="/images/542/542-dante-out.png"
