The optional **StudioBox HD3** and **HD5** accessories centralize the Studio monitoring connections (headphones and main speakers).

![StudioBox HD5 - HD3](/images/accesorios/HD5-HD3.png)

Features:

- HD5: 5 headphone outputs with independent volume controls
- HD3: 3 headphone outputs
- Dual listening channels
- Monitor output for the Studio main speakers with level knob on the panel.
- ON AIR tally light
- Reverse talkback from Studio to Control Room
- Timer/Clock (HD5 only)
