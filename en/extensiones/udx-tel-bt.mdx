The UDX TEL+BT adapter provides connection for one ****landline telephone and Bluetooth link**** for a mobile phone.

- For console connection, configuration and landline adjustment [proceed as in the UDX 2TEL model](/en/unidex/UDX-2TEL).
- The procedure for pairing and using a mobile phone [is explained in the TELCO section](/en/unidex/ux24-op-telco).
