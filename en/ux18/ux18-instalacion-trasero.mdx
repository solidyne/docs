## Power Supply

UX18 uses an external switched-mode power supply, 110/240V 50/60Hz. The power supply connector is located on the rear panel.

<Danger>
  First connect the threaded connector to the rear panel, then connect the power supply to the mains.
</Danger>

## Grounding

UX24 is grounded through the power cable. Do not use adapters or cables that bypass the ground pin. It is also recommended to connect the chassis ground terminal to the facility's ground using 4 mm cable or larger.

The general electrical installation must have proper grounding. For reference, you can check the [FM ground connections diagram](/es/puesta-a-tierra).

<Danger>
  **WARNING!**

  Make sure that any computer connected to UX24 via USB has proper grounding.
</Danger>

## Rear Panel

The rear panel contains all console connections. RJ45 connectors are used for all audio connections, except for microphone inputs, headphone outputs and control room speaker outputs.

You will also find **Ethernet** ports, **USB** port, **Tally Light** connections and **GPIO** control.

<Icon icon="arrow-right-to-line" iconType="solid" color="#008dff" size={22} />  [INPUTS](/es/unidex/instalacion-entradas) (click for more info)

- 5 microphone inputs with XLR connectors.
- 8 mono UNIDEX inputs groupable in stereo pairs.
- 2 balanced stereo.
- 1 unbalanced stereo "On-Air Monitor" (assignable to a channel).
- 2 AES3 digital.
- 4 USB stereo audio.
- 1 audio streaming (replaces AES3-1)
- OPTION: DANTE x16

- 2 balanced PGM
- 4 balanced stereo
- 2 AES3 digital
- 4 USB stereo audio
- 2 audio streams
- OPTION: DANTE x16
