<Accordion title="Audio Inputs">
  - 4 microphone inputs (3 for dynamic + 1 built-in condenser).
  - 1 mono line input (portable recorders).
  - USB computer connection (stereo input + Studio return for Internet broadcasting).
  - MIC input level: -20 dBu to -75 dBu.
  - Non-saturable microphone preamplifiers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Audio Outputs">
  - Balanced output: 0 dBu / 10 kΩ.
  - USB for Internet broadcasting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Telephone Hybrid">
  - 2 outputs with internal hybrid.
  - Landline (POTS) and cellular phone via Bluetooth.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Headphones">
  - 3 headphone outputs.
  - Impedance: 8 to 600 Ω.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Audio Performance">
  - Frequency response: 20 – 15,000 Hz flat.
  - Anti-pop filter: –6 dB @ 30 Hz.
  - Low-pass filter: –3 dB @ 12 kHz.
  - Signal-to-noise ratio: 70 dBA (mic level –40 dBu).
  - THD: \< 0.4 % @ 1 kHz.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Processing">
  - Audio compressor with 16 dB range and 2 LED indicator.
    - Attack: 10 ms
    - Release: 200 ms
  - Ultra-fast peak limiter after compression stage, prevents clipping during transmission.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Technology and Battery Life">
  - Ultra-low power CMOS amplifiers.
  - Cell phone connection: wireless Bluetooth or "hands-free" adapter cable.
  - Battery charger: multi-voltage 90–240 V adapter (USB).
  - Battery life: up to 16 hours continuous operation.
  - Charging time: overnight (~12 h).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Dimensions and Weight">
  - Dimensions: 121 × 129 × 37 mm
  - Weight: 600 g (0.6 kg)
</Accordion>
