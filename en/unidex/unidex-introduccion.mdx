## Introduction

The Solidyne **UNIDEX** line covers the operational and broadcasting needs of FM, AM and streaming stations. UNIDEX offers a digital workflow without the complexity of a dedicated IP network, while providing audio over IP capability (DANTE/AES67).

## UDX Adapters

- **UNIDEX** stands for "UNIversal Desktop with EXternal adapters".
- Each UNIDEX input accepts a UDX adapter (telecommunications, microphones, USB, etc.).
- Direct connection to console: no routers, switches or networks required.
- Adapters communicate data and function control from the console surface.

## Audio over IP (optional)

1. Enables advanced interconnection between devices and studios through an **audio network**.
2. Requires a **dedicated network** and compatible DANTE/AES67 input/output nodes.
3. Greater flexibility for setting up remotely controlled studios via web interface.

## Hardware

- Internal processing: Full-digital DSP **24 bits @ 48 kHz**.
- High-durability 100mm soft-touch faders:\
  UX24: 12 physical + 12 "virtual" on 7" touchscreen\
  UX18: 10 physical + 8 "virtual" on 7" touchscreen
- UX24 expandable to 18 or 24 physical faders with UX6 panels (6 faders).

## On-Air Operation

- **Plug & play**: factory configuration ready to use.
- No advanced knowledge required.
- Operation similar to traditional console.
- Audio processing adjusted on touchscreen.

## User Levels

1. **Basic**: access to minimal functions.
2. **Intermediate**: includes advanced options.
3. **Administrator**: full access to configuration and settings.

## Advanced Flexibility

- Assign **any input** to any physical or virtual fader.
- Create **scenes** with channel and processing settings.
- **Remote** control and configuration via IP (WEB browser).
- Supports up to two simultaneous on-air studios (UX24 model).
- Use **VST effects** in real-time via USB ASIO and external computer.

<Info>
  All functions and settings are managed from the touchscreen or via IP from a tablet or computer browser.
