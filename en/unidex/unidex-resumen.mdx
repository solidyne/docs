The Solidyne **UNIDEX** line covers operational and broadcasting needs for **FM, AM and streaming stations**. UNIDEX offers a digital workflow without the complexity of a dedicated IP network, while providing IP audio capability (DANTE/AES67).

![Unidex Line Pn](/images/thumbnails/Linea_unidex.png)

## Models

The UNIDEX Series includes two mixing console models: **UX24** and **UX18**. Both models share features and operating modes.

<Note>
  To simplify documentation, common aspects are explained in this section. The [UX24](/es/ux24/ux24-resumen) and [UX18](/es/ux18/ux18-resumen) entries detail the operational and configuration specifics of each model.
</Note>

---

## UDX Adapters

- **UNIDEX** stands for "UNIversal Desktop with EXternal adapters".
- Each UNIDEX input accepts a UDX adapter (telecommunications, microphones, USB, etc.).
- Direct connection to console: no routers, switches or networks required.
- Adapters communicate data and function control from the console surface.

---

## IP Audio (optional)

1. Enables advanced interconnection between devices and studios through an **audio network**.
2. Requires a **dedicated network** and compatible DANTE/AES67 input/output nodes.
3. Control via WEB interface from remote studios.

---

## Hardware

- Internal processing: **24-bit @ 48 kHz** full-digital DSP.
- High-durability 100mm soft-touch faders:\
  UX24: 12 mechanical + 12 "virtual" on 7" touchscreen\
  UX18: 10 mechanical + 8 "virtual" on 7" touchscreen
- UX24 expandable to 18 or 24 mechanical faders with UX6 panels (6 faders).

## Broadcasting

- **Plug & play**: factory configuration ready to use.
- No advanced knowledge required.
- Operation similar to traditional console.
- Audio processing adjusted via touchscreen.

---

## User Levels

1. **Basic**: access to minimal functions.
2. **Intermediate**: includes advanced options.
3. **Administrator**: full access to configuration and settings.

---

## Advanced Flexibility

- Assign **any input** to any of the 12 mechanical faders or 12 virtual faders.
- Create **scenes** with channel and processing settings.
- **Remote** control and configuration via IP (WEB browser).
- Supports up to two simultaneous air studios.
- Use **VST effects** in real-time via USB ASIO and external computer.

<Info>
  All functions and adjustments are managed from the touchscreen or via IP from a browser on tablet or computer.
</Info>
