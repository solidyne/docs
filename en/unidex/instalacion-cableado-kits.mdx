## Pre-assembled Cable Kits

Solidyne offers cable kits and adapters designed to connect the console without the need to assemble cables. The options are:

- Generic pre-assembled cable kit for new installations.
- Generic adapter kit to connect UNIDEX in pre-existing installations.
- Custom kit configured by user.
